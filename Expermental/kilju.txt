# **10‑Litre Fermented Drink Recipe (Scaled Version)**

**Original recipe (1 L):**  
- 500 ml warm water  
- 500 ml cold water  
- 5 crushed raisins  
- 170 g sugar  
- 1 tea bag  
- 1 teaspoon yeast  

**10‑litre scaled recipe:**  
- **5 litres warm water**  
- **5 litres cold water**  
- **50 crushed raisins**  
- **1.7 kg sugar**  
- **10 tea bags**  
- **10 teaspoons yeast (32–35 grams)**

this expect you to know how to open your terminal and have pyinstaller installed on your system

copy and paste the WHOLE thing FROM " to the other " but do not inclue the "'s
and have the files listed in the same folder
and make sure your terminal is in the folder.

this make one app on mac or exe on windows.

mac:
"pyinstaller --noconsole --onefile --icon=phoenix.png \
  --add-data "phoenix.png:." \
  --add-data "rankmessages.txt:." \
  --add-data "trainers.txt:." \
  --add-data "specialphrases.txt:." \
  rc27.py"

linux:
"pyinstaller --noconsole --onefile --icon=phoenix.png \
  --add-data "phoenix.png:." \
  --add-data "rankmessages.txt:." \
  --add-data "trainers.txt:." \
  --add-data "specialphrases.txt:." \
  rc27.py"
  
windows via terminal(win key + x): 
"pyinstaller --noconsole --onefile --icon=phoenix.png --add-data "phoenix.png;." --add-data "rankmessages.txt;." --add-data "trainers.txt;." --add-data "specialphrases.txt;." rc27.py"

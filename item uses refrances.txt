belt of the wild:
The belt of the wild allows Rangers to change shape & befriend creatures.
Type “/use /reflect” to check your knowledge of creatures.
Type “/use /befriend” to befriend a creature you have studied in depth.
Type “/use /call” to call a friendly creature to return to you.
Type “/use /control” to directly control your friend.
Type “/use /dismiss” to dismiss a creature back to the wild.
Type “/use /shape <name>” to assume the shape of a creature you have studied extensively.Type “/use /return” to return to your normal form.
Type “/use /judge <player>” to judge another's progress along the path to becoming a Ranger.
Type “/use /track <name>” to track a nearby creature which you have studied.

Bloodblade:
This weapon can be used to channel your life energies into your blows.
Type “/use /reflect” to check your training with this tool.
Type “/use /practice” to train yourself to better use this tool.
Type “/use /control” to start controlling the powers of your bloodblade. Type “/use /control” again to unleash the blade’s full power.
Type “/use /hamstring” to hamstring the next opponent you hit.
Type “/use /cripple” to cripple the next opponent you hit.
Type “/use /disable” to disable the next opponent you hit.
Add “/cancel” to the end of a command to cancel a toggled ability, or “/on” to activate it.
Type “/use /absorb” to absorb the essence of a creature.
Type “/use /absorb /report” to review what creatures you have absorbed.
Type “/use /purge <creature name>” to purge your blade of the essence of <creature name>.
Or just “/use /purge all” to purge all creatures you have absorbed.

Bloodstone ring:
* This tool is a focus used in elementary blood magic.
* /use to toggle the powers of the ring.
* /use /on or /use /off to activate or deactivate the powers of the ring.
* /set to set or reset the ring's health limit to your current health.
* /set <number>% to set the ring's health limit to a percent of your maximum health.
* /clear to clear your previously set health limit.
* Note: /clear has no effect on your current health limit.
* /use /dead to feign death.
* Unequip the ring or say the magic word to cease feigning death.
* The current magic word is ''.
* Type '/use /magicword' followed by the magic word to change it. For example, /use /magicword Thoom

Sungem:
Your sunstone allows you to think messages to others telepathically.
/THINK <msg> to broadcast a message to everyone
/THINKTO <name> <msg> to send a private message to one person
Do not include spaces or punctuation in the <name>.
For example: "hermajesty", not "her majesty"
/THINKCLAN <msg> to broadcast a message to everyone in your clan
/THINKGROUP <msg> to broadcast a message to everyone you have added to your group
/useitem sungem /group ? to see how to set up your group
/useitem sungem /inspect to check the condition of your sungem
